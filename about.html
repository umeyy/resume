🌷 About Me – Beyond the First Glance

Hey, I’m Habiba M. Fazil — a third-year Software Engineering student, a girl from Matiari, and someone whose strength is rooted not just in code, but in silent resilience, love, and dreams far bigger than herself.

If you’ve made it here, welcome to the most personal page on this site — not just what I do, but who I am.

🌿 Where I Come From

I grew up in a small city called Matiari, raised by the strongest woman I know — my mom.

My father passed away before I was even born. Since then, it’s always been just the two of us. My mother has been my world: my strength, my softness, my love, my everything. Every line of code I write, every goal I chase, every late-night dream — it’s all for her.

She supported me emotionally.
I supported us financially.
I'm proud to say: I’ve earned every step of my journey.

💡 My Shift – From Silence to Spark

I wasn’t always like this. Once extroverted, I now live somewhere between quiet and expressive — an ambivert with a deep mind and a soft heart.

Some days I’m full of energy and laughter. Other days, I’m just the quiet girl again — headphones in, avoiding noise, floating in thoughts. Mood defines me. Music grounds me.

I’m also deeply private. Most people don't know what I carry, or what I’ve been through. But if you follow my work… or my social media… you might just see it.

It’s not built yet. But it's alive in me.

Something big. Something just mine. Something for her.
